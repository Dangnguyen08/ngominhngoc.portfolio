The Buffet Party

Description

  • The Buffet Party is a curated project designated to orientate personal hobbies and passions for high school students and undergraduates.
  • At TBP, participants could engage in various simulations of common hobbies through 12 realistic booth modules to dictate desirable career paths
  • The event attracted 250 aspiring students and earned coverage from 10+ news report outlets.

Problems

  • Initial lack of human resources heavily undermined working spirits
  • Lack of fundamental knowledge and prior experience deterred event operation.
  • Lack of visionary leadership, organizational strategies and a clear implementation plans paralyzed working culture.
  • Weak business partner network inhibited sponsorship solicitation.
  • Lack of standard proposals lessened the prospects of successful solicitation.
  • Budget deficiency that potentially led to event cancellation.
  • Financial hardship (caused by lockdown) hindered agencies from offering financial support, team members failed to sign sponsored deals.

    • Solutions

  • Recruited 40 new members
  • Customized intensive coach combined with practical case study through planning, experimenting with marketing strategy to revitalize the operating process,..
  • Reorganized and overhauled various working departments, each with defined specifications.
  • Identified, developed and cultivated a warm contact hitlist of 20 potential sponsors working in education.
  • Actively asked for materials from reliable sources.
  • Generated adequate funding through selling foods and beverages on the Walking street.
  • Actively contacted different parties and set up meeting to earn sponsoring deals. Actively revamped common mistakes to increase efficiency in future negotiation.

    • Problems

  • Declining team’s morale disunited working spirits Inexperience in orchestrating events culminated numerous issues (struggles to pin banners, distorted decorations due to failure in property management) .

    • Solutions

  • Improvised problem-solving

  • Experience in recruiting, training, supervising, and cultivating a positive working culture aligned with the organization’s core values. Evaluating and adopting personal promotion for member demonstrating decent performance.
  • Experience in developing visionary leadership, creating clear and long-term organizational strategies with deliverable and measurable goals.
  • Experience in documenting professional business proposal and clients offers.
