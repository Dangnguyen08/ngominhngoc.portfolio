Zalo Connect

Description

In the event of unprecedented Covid resurgence, on 04/08/2021, Zalo connect was introduced to streamline quick access to vulnerable individuals in needs of essential reliefs, food supplies and medical aid. Resonated with the national solidarity spirits, I noticed few collective attempts have been made by students to forge effective solutions.Thus, I see this as the opportunity to motivate aspiring youngsters proactively taking parts in supporting hard-hit victims mentally and physically.

After 2 weeks of devising plans and delegating human resources, on 23rd of August 2021, our project officially kickstarted. Through coordination with VietAbroader Hanoi/ Vinschool Harmony H2H and effective execution, we managed to exceed 50 essential relief packages - [estimated worth varied from 300.000 to 1.000.000 VND each] that encompassed food parcels (rice, milk, noodle), hygiene packs, medical supplies (test kit, face-mask) and a humble sum of financial aid. Furthermore, each bundle received was bonused with 300.000 VND, taken directly from our previous business operation’s profits.

After 5 days in operation, due to booming requests and overwhelmingly generous offers from donors, VA HN launched an appeal account with a view to spreading positive messages and safeguarding aiding nets that reached even more Covid-strickens.

Overall, we had single-handedly raised over 10.000.000 VND - all of which were given to destitute people.

Media-wise, the event was endorsed and promoted by 30 micro influencers - young professionals with well-targeted popularity and hands-on expertise in organizing events for the youth.

Our social welfare project also earned Vinschool’s coverage.

The project ceased operating abruptly due to a lack of proper preparation, brought about by the emergency of the surging wave. Stil, our coordinated efforts with VA Hanoi and Vinschool H2H had exceeded prior expectations to achieve significant social outreach:
  • Delivered over 100 gift packages to a total of 60 families in needs.
  • Empower proactive acts of communal good-will through the spread of positive and full-filling messages.
  • Gained another 1000 subscribers on VA HN official fanpage within a radical few weeks.
