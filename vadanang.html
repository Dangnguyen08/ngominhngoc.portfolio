VietAbroader Danang

Description

    With a view to inspiring and unlocking true potentials of youngsters, VietAbroader Danang was founded in 2021 to forge our mission further to students in Danang, who strive for a scholarship in prestigious universities. With constant efforts, we have held a vocational orientation sequence program to:

  • 1. Forster an insight into personal exploration and development so that students can opt for the right path.
  • 2. Counsel a suitable career path that matches up a student's identity and capability, supporting students with a desire to study abroad.

    • On this journey, we have talked to 1500+ high schoolers in Danang, with an aim to broaden their mindset towards the gravity of choosing the right pathway and building a community to support further in their university admission progress.
