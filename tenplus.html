Ten Plus

Description

  • Ten plus is a curated program designated to welcome and orientate CNN’s 10 graders.
  • Ten plus aims at providing warranted mental and physical aids for 10 grader and creating extra opportunities to foster connections between sophomores and fresh-man.
  • With over 1200 participants, 10 plus is CNN’s annual and traditional event.
  • Work terms: 3 months.
  • Position: Orientation group leader.

  • During 2 months of preparation for Camp day, I had actively proposed 10+ revitalization and reformulation yet received only dismissal for the following reasons.
  • Ten plus is an annual event with an outdated procedure of unchanging activities, unchanging operations reused throughout 13 years. With a prevalently fixed and conservative mindset, perpetuating and upholding long-held traditions were excused for problem-blind and unwelcoming attitudes toward innovations.

  • Robust innovations alone could not render radical changes in the working procedures and long-held systems despite their glaring problems.
  • I recognized the momentum of managing, cultivating a liberating work culture where members are encouraged to usher in changes and freely contribute their voices to policy-making. I also noted the significance of strategic negotiation skills.
  • I could not resent my opinions to tune in with the crowds and passively accept problem-blind as others had done. Therefore, after futile attempts to drive changes, I realized that this is not the environment where I can thrive. I persisted in leaving and seeking a different environment where my potential could be better harnessed.
