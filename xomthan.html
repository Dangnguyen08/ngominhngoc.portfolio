Xom Than

Description

With a view to creating real value through giving and endowment, I had volunteered to donate an allocation of profits derived from prior business schemes to the destitute in Xóm Thận (Hanoi and Dien Bien) during the 2022 Tet holiday (organized by Vinschool H2H).

  • Empowered proactive acts of communal goodwill through spreading positivity.
  • Collaborated with Vinschool Heart2Heart in donating 40 million VND to 46 people on dialysis in Hanoi and Ngoc Hoi.
  • Relieved and alleviated financial burdens for those in need.
  • Provided mental aids that cultivated holiday fulfillment.
  • Further emphasized our missionary and visionary through working to serve the community.
