BAO FAIR

BAO'S Description

Entering the tenth grade proved a great milestone of mine - driven by the zestful spirits of aspiring peers, I persisted in securing myself a position at “Team CNN leaders” - CNN’s leading society at fostering progressive change-makers. At Team CNN leaders, I was given the chance to orchestrate Bao - a nine-year-old vocational fair where students can experience various job modules through realistic simulation booths.

  • In 2021, Bao had attracted over 300 passionate participants to attend and experience its 12 booths’ simulations of top sought-after professions.
  • Work term: 10 months
  • Position: Member of Finance Department

  • Initially, I received intensive training on theoretical fiscal management, sponsorship appeals (via cold calls, cold emails), targeted pitch deck, and contract constructions. However, my early attempts at soliciting financial offers got dismissed, due to hit lists being ill-targeted
  • Out of >100 sponsors delineated in hit lists, my team managed to generate 10.000.000 VND including monetary and non-monetary terms.
  • I had conducted numerous cold calls harnessing pre-mentored techniques, but still being repeatedly rejected.
  • Dissatisfied and unwavered, I proactively seek consultations and hands-on experience from a senior with prior expertise in directing a 1000-spectator concert. While earning viable experience, I still failed at driving results

  • Experience in constructing fiscally sound budgetary management
  • Experience in crafting a list of 20 potential sponsors, developing the pitch deck, and successfully converting 2 of them into partners with total funding of 10.000.0000 VND
  • Experience in soliciting sponsorship through telemarketing (via cold calls, warm calls)
  • Experience in fostering and strengthening community relationships by leading efforts to create 2 long-term partnerships and cross-business collaborations.
  • Financial visions: Despite gaining mostly fundamental understandings, I awarded fulfillment through developing a unique visionary. In my observance, high school students’ limited understanding of business interests and lack of hands-on experience often resulted in the failure to offer a win-win scenario. Therefore, I am determined to provide visionary leadership by rethinking a strategic model that addresses measurable goals 1. What values do business partners look for 2. What values can the student community transpire 3. How to come up with strategies that harmonize the needs and the viability.
